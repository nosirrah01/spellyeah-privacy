# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 1, 2026

Spell Yeah is a free Android word puzzle game published by **Super Fun App Builder Guy**. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the app.

Spell Yeah does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location.

However, Spell Yeah does include ads through Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may collect or receive certain information as described below.

## Information We Collect

Spell Yeah does not currently have its own backend server, and we do not directly collect personal information from users through an account system or sign-up form.

The app may store basic game progress locally on your device, such as:

* Coins
* Level progress
* Other basic game settings or progress data

Because the app includes Google AdMob, Google may collect or receive certain information through the Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and app performance purposes.

This may include information such as:

* Device identifiers
* Advertising ID
* Approximate location estimated from IP address
* Ad interactions
* Diagnostics
* Crash and performance data

We do not sell personal data.

## Local Game Data

Spell Yeah stores basic game progress locally on your device using browser or local device storage.

This local game data is used to keep track of your progress in the game, such as coins and level progress.

This data is not sent to our own server, because Spell Yeah currently does not have its own backend server.

If you uninstall the app, clear app data, or reset your device storage, your local game progress may be lost.

## Advertising

Spell Yeah is a free app supported by ads.

The app uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK to show ads. Ad types may include:

* Banner ads
* Interstitial ads
* Rewarded ads

Google AdMob may use collected or received data to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, improve services, and comply with legal requirements.

Google’s use of data is handled according to Google’s own policies.

You can learn more here:

[How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Third-Party Services

Spell Yeah uses the following third-party service:

* Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK

Third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We do not control how Google collects or uses data through AdMob.

## Children’s Privacy

Spell Yeah is not directed to children under 13.

The app does not require users to create an account, and we do not knowingly ask children to provide personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, camera access, microphone access, or precise location.

Because Spell Yeah uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK to show ads, parents and guardians should review Google’s privacy practices and available ad settings.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

## Data Retention

Basic game progress may remain stored locally on your device until you delete the app, clear app data, or otherwise remove the stored data.

We do not currently store game progress on our own server.

Data collected or received by Google AdMob is handled according to Google’s own retention policies.

## User Choices

You can control some data and ad settings through your device and Google settings.

Depending on your device and Android version, you may be able to:

* Reset or delete your advertising ID
* Limit ad personalization
* Clear app data
* Uninstall the app

Clearing app data or uninstalling the app may delete your local game progress.
